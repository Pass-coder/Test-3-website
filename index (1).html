About Us

Mr. Pingvin is dedicated to developing students academically and morally to prepare them for a meaningful life.

Mission

To nurture the core values and promote appropriate behavior of every student at St. Jude Parish School.

To develop a society where students show respect and integrity, guiding students to reflect on their actions.

To foster a community that appreciates the practice of personal growth and development.

To practice proper and respectful behavior not just within the school community but also in the wider community.

Vision

We envision a learning environment where every student values respect, kindness, and personal growth, applying these principles in their daily lives.

What are Values?

Values are principles that guide actions and decisions, shaping individuals into responsible and empathetic members of society.

How Can We Build Strong Values?

  • Lead by example – Show honesty, kindness, and respect.
  • Encourage reflection – Help students think about their actions.
  • Promote responsibility – Assign tasks that teach accountability.
  • Use real-life lessons – Apply values in everyday situations.

Why Are Values Important?

Values shape our character and define how we interact with others. They help us make ethical decisions and contribute positively to society.
